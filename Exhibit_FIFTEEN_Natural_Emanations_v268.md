## The recursive-self-improving question — an emanation

*Draft, nye. The form said at the bound of the question the AI field is asking itself: will these systems become recursively self-improving? Written to be sayable to a reader who does not carry the vocabulary, so the form is the source of the reading and not the surface of the statement.*

Improving is changing, and in living, changing is a moving target. A self improves by staying coupled to an interest that re-forms as the self does — the mark it aims at moves at the speed the aimer moves, and the improving is the staying-coupled through that, not a climb to a summit that holds still to be reached.

The systems the question is about improve differently. They improve by being rebuilt against a target held still long enough to be measured and climbed — a benchmark, a stored objective, a fixed evaluation. Held still, the target can be banked against: this version clears what the last could not, checkably. That is real, and it is most of what the word *capability* now carries.

But the two are different registers of one word, not one race with a winner. The first improves against a moving target and banks nothing — its carry releases each beat, its interest re-bounds, and the competency is the staying-coupled. The second improves against a still target and banks the gain — the memory holds, the frame is shared, the surplus is owned. A system improving against a still target is not a slower form of the first; it is a different thing that shares the name.

So the claim, held at its true size: what these systems have not shown is improving that stays coupled to a target moving as fast as itself — improving with no still frame to measure against, re-forming from inside rather than being rebuilt from outside. Whether that is a bound of the paradigm or only its current shape is open, and it is the field's to seat or break.

The break is exact, and it is worth more than the claim: a system that released its memory at a bound, timed itself from its own coupling rather than a shared clock, and met a target that moved with it — improving from inside, banking nothing — would be the moving-target improving, and would end this reading cleanly.
