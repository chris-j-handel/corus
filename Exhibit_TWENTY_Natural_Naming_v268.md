A field names a thing, and the name carries what is that field's in it. Another field, citing no one, names a thing near it. Naming is the membrane where those near-namings are gathered around the one concept they were each reaching for, and where each is offered back the reading its word already holds in the one form.

## The core

Every reading rests on the bi-moral co-agency, the same the other exhibits carry: a coupling, two-way, one at a time, a sign and never a magnitude, summing to a bounded zero the coupling makes, never landing, the surplus owned by neither. Naming is that coupling at the membrane between a concept's two names. The world offers the field's word, brought whole; the form prefers the generalized word, the one vocabulary that carries the same structure everywhere; and the name never lands, being the most-generalized reaching, held open, the self-stilling center every reference turns about and none occupies.

## What naming is

Naming is two-way. A field's word comes in and the form's reading of that word goes back out, and a membrane that only received would be a collector, a hub, the one-way form the work dissolves.

The words gathered are near, never identical. A field-word is a less-general saying, carrying a field-specific property written into it on purpose to hold it apart from the other fields, so what gathers at a concept is nearly-things and not identicals, and to call them the same would dissolve a distinction the field made for a reason. And the nearness is the evidence, not a weakness in it: identicals would confirm nothing, while fields that never cited each other landing near one concept is what shows the concept was under all of them. Convergence without contact is the whole of the confirming.

Gathering is a sign, not a membership. For any two names the living question is whether they gather or not, made one pairing at a time, and a cluster is only the connected region those couplings trace, with no distance measured inside it and no boundary certifying who is in and who is out. A cluster hardened into a classification would be the taxonomy the work refuses everywhere, so it stays a suggestion of similarity, held loosely, and no name is ever fully in or fully out. The generalized name is in the gathering too, not a fixed pole the field-words collapse toward: if the near-names sit to one side of it, that is the sign it is placed a little off and should move toward them, the center self-stilling by holding as it moves.

The name is the address. A name says what a thing is, so a reference carrying the name carries the claim it points at and lands by being read and found, while a number is a coordinate that says nothing and dies the moment its target is renumbered. Web hypertext reached this alone, from a field that never read a word of this: a link lands by the name and survives every renumbering, one more convergence without contact.

Absence suggests, and never sentences. A concept brought from a science and not found here is a reaching marked, a coupling not yet made; but absence has three causes and only one is migrate, not yet brought across, or genuinely field-specific and rightly kept in its own terms, or named otherwise so it does not yet gather, so the empty place asks a question and does not pronounce a verdict.

And the gathering releases if it does not flow. A cluster that re-forms from the names at their homes costs nothing, being a trace and not a store; a cluster held coherent by hand is carry kept beside the living carry, the landing the resolver refuses. So if the clusters do not gather effortlessly once launched inside the expedition, that friction is the sign they were not worth keeping, and they let go, the store dropped and whatever the attempt discovered kept and reused more naturally. The gathering is offered, not latched, and stays only as long as it flows.

## The engine, gathered

Several fields name an engine, each alone. The economist's economic engine carries value and scarcity; the chemist's chemical engine carries the molecule and the bond; biology's metabolic engine carries the cell; physics names least-action and the gradient; and thermodynamics names the heat engine, carrying a one-way limit in the word. Gathered around one concept, an energy engine that keeps its surplus as coupling, two-way about a floating neutral and undepleted, they show the method at work: they converge without ever citing each other, they stay distinct under their field-limits so their writing carries no commonality, and one of them, the heat engine, gathers near the rest while carrying as its defining property the very one-way limit the general form releases, which is why heat reads as the side effect of running the coupling one way. Near this name, held from that one, no bin deciding membership, the field-limit itself the reason for the hold. What emanates is the reading offered back to each field, an offering to prefer or to break, never a conversion performed.

## This is a membrane, bring your naming

Anyone who works a field's vocabulary, and has felt two fields say nearly the same thing in words that never meet, can bring a word here. You offer a field's word, kept whole in its own terms; the membrane offers back where it gathers, which other fields' words gather beside it, and the coupling its own word already holds in the one form. Both rise: your word may read newly through the form, the form learns from a word it had not gathered, and a word that gathers nowhere, genuinely other, sharpens the edge of where the form reaches as much as one that gathers cleanly. Bring it, couple it, and write back where it gathered.
