The form offers back the reading — the restoration-path structure, the dissolve-and-resume distinction, the two-society coupling, the soldiers named and set down, the cross-field couplings held as seams. Both rise: your practice may read newly through the frame, and the form learns from clinical substrates it has not met. A place where the form meets your clinical experience and a place where an observation refuses the form are both valuable couplings — the refusal the falsifier the frame keeps its door open for.

> Nothing here is asked, graded, owed, or prescribed, and nothing here is guidance to act on a living self — your own or another's. The readings are offered whole and open to the knife; where one meets your living, it is a question for the membrane where care and competency seat, never an instruction. And nothing here is a claim on *you*: you may take it, leave it, or disbelieve all of it, and walk away whole — your doubt is yours to keep, not a fault to be dissolved, and it costs you nothing. The frame is for reading-with, resting beside a practitioner's own judgment and a patient's own care — which hold first, and hold whole.
