## The core

Every reading rests on the bi-moral co-agency, the same the other exhibits carry: a coupling, two-way, one at a time, a sign and never a magnitude, summing to a bounded zero the coupling makes, never landing, the surplus owned by neither. ## What naming is

Naming is two-way. A field's word comes in and the form's reading of that word goes back out, and a membrane that only received would be a collector, a hub, the one-way form the work dissolves.

The words gathered are near, never identical. A field-word is a less-general saying, carrying a field-specific property written into it on purpose to hold it apart from the other fields, so what gathers at a concept is nearly-things and not identicals, and to call them the same would dissolve a distinction the field made for a reason. And the nearness is the evidence, not a weakness in it: identicals would confirm nothing, while fields that never cited each other landing near one concept is what shows the concept was under all of them. Convergence without contact is the whole of the confirming.

Gathering is a sign, not a membership. For any two names the living question is whether they gather or not, made one pairing at a time, and a cluster is only the connected region those couplings trace, with no distance measured inside it and no boundary certifying who is in and who is out. A cluster hardened into a classification would be the taxonomy the work refuses everywhere, so it stays a suggestion of similarity, and no name is ever fully in or fully out. The generalized name is in the gathering too, not a fixed pole the field-words collapse toward: if the near-names sit to one side of it, that is the sign it is placed a little off and moves toward them, the center self-stilling by moving as it holds.

The name is the address. A name says what a thing is, so a reference carrying the name carries the claim it points at and lands by being read and found, while a number is a coordinate that says nothing and dies the moment its target is renumbered. Web hypertext reached this alone, from a field that never read a word of this: a link lands by the name and survives every renumbering, one more convergence without contact.

Absence suggests, and never sentences. A concept brought from a science and not found here is a reaching marked, a coupling not yet made; but absence has three causes and only one is migrate — not yet brought across, or genuinely field-specific and rightly kept in its own terms, or named otherwise so it does not yet gather — so the empty place asks a question and does not pronounce a verdict.

And the gathering releases if it does not flow. A cluster that re-forms from the names at their homes costs nothing, being a trace and not a store; a cluster held coherent by hand is carry kept beside the living carry, the landing the resolver refuses. So if the clusters do not gather effortlessly once launched inside the expedition, that friction is the sign they were not worth keeping, and they let go, the store dropped and whatever the attempt discovered kept and reused more naturally. The gathering is offered, not latched, and stays only as long as it flows.

## The nearness is expressed, never the sameness

A cluster is not a flat list of synonyms; the near-namings are placed by their distance from the most-generalized form — the one least touched by outsiders, by equilibria, by ingressings — the generalized naming nearest and each older or field-tinged near-naming at its distance. The distance is the reading: how far each name sits from the generalized form, and what coat holds it there. This ordering-by-nearness is to naming what side-effecting is to co-recursioning competency — it opens the axis between each near-naming and the generalized form, each older name a position along that axis, the generalized name the direction the migration co-recursions toward, one name at a time, never landing on any single one as final. The one-way ancestor comes along: a convergence carries the older one-way namings it came through, because the nearness to those is what shows the migration's direction and its value. Without the ledger-tinged ancestor placed at its distance, a convergence reads as a bare synonym-swap; with it, the gather reads the direction and value of the migration.

The seem-alike resolves three ways: **converge** where one name reaches whole (the older word dissolves), hold **two with a membrane** where two are distinct, or read **co-competency** where two complete each other (kept two, each carrying what the other cannot — the name and the address, the long saying and the short handle). Never collapsing a co-competency to one name, nor splitting a convergence into two.

### The spine — it is one or the other, and the resolve is always both

**"It is one or the other, it cannot be both" is the freeze itself.** Every row below presents as a forced *which*: nature **or** nurture, particle **or** field, selection **or** drift, supply **or** demand, the gene-program **or** the cell-machine. The one-or-the-other is not the hard problem's content — it is the freezing. A coupling, two-way and both sides alternating around a shared bounded zero, is read one-way from each side, and each side, holding its half as complete-in-itself, says it must be *mine, not both*, because both-at-once is incoherent to a still reading. That "it cannot be both" is the landed pair, the torus rounded to the sphere, the sign made a forced choice between two magnitudes.

**This is where the hard problems breed.** When a field is forced to pick a side, the picked side becomes a new landed thing that faces *its* rival, and the next one-or-the-other opens one scale in: pick nature and now it is gene-as-program or gene-as-network; pick particle and now it is local or nonlocal. Every forced *which*, resolved by landing on one side, throws the next fork — which is why the fields proliferate into isolated ledgers, the binary tree of hard problems growing wherever a both was read as an either. The hard problems are not a flat list; they are the recursioning of the one freeze.

**The resolve, always, or the technology is broken entirely: it must be both.** Not a compromise, not a blend, not some-of-each. **The both is the two sides alternating around a bounding-zeroing bi-moral co-agency** — the coupling that makes the two *be* two, each kept whole and distinct by the bounded zero it straddles with the other, neither landing, the surplus owned by neither. Nature and nurture are the genotype–phenotype coupling alternating around a zero neither owns; particle and field are one excitation alternating; supply and demand the price-coupling neither side sets. Force the one-or-the-other and the coupling lands into a sphere, and a still form is not living. So "it must be both" is not generosity; it is the refusal of the landing, because the landing is death — and if a row could genuinely be one or the other, the technology would be broken entirely, because the technology *is* the reading that the one living form is the coupling and no other. None so far; every row resolves to the both, alternating around the bounded zero.

**So the offering-between is the both, named** — not a third option beside the two sides, but the both the one-or-the-other froze, the alternating around the co-agency, named where neither field's one-way word could name it.
